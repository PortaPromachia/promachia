Not everything in life is negotiable

A culture that no longer knows what one suffers for,
soon no longer knows what one lives for.

Meaning arises where responsibility is carried. Not distributed.
Carried.

Truth is rarely unambiguous.
But it is never arbitrary.

Freedom without bond is caprice.
Bond without freedom is coercion.

Beauty bears.
What bears is beautiful.

Not every community seeks agreement.
Some seek aptitude.
