Inquiry

Encounter is not an event but a state. It arises where concentration becomes possible because selection has already occurred. Time here is not a frame but a stake. What is read does not remain theory. What is thought does not remain without consequence. One does not come to watch. One stays to work: on matters, on oneself, together.
